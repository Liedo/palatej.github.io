# Notations

The following notations are used in all the algorithms: 

$$ a_{t+k|t}=E\left(\alpha_{t+k} | y_0 \cdots y_{t}\right)$$  

$$ P_{t+k|t}=var\left(\alpha_{t+k} | y_0 \cdots y_{t}\right)$$  

$$ a_{t+1}=a_{t+1|t}, \: P_{t+1}=P_{t+1|t} $$
